Chandni Shah

In Loving Memory

1960-2005

"The loss of Professor Shah leaves a void that cannot be filled."
Her devotion and dedication to our students and their education was unparalleled. Her generosity and kindness to all of us will never be forgotten."

- Dr. Erwin Lutwak, Head of the Department of Mathematics.

On April 9, 2005 Dr. Chandni Shah, Industry Associate Professor and Deputy Head of the Department of Mathematics, passed away at the age of 45.
Dr. Shah joined Poly in 1997, becoming the Director of Freshman Mathematics and, later, Deputy Head of the Department of Mathematics.Before coming to Poly, Dr. Shah held faculty positions at University of California at Riverside and Colgate University.Dr. Shah earned a PhD in Mathematics from the University of Texas. She received her bachelor's and master's degrees in mathematics from University of Illinois at Chicago.
She is survived by her mother, her two brothers, and their families.
